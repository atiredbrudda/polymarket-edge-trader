<objective>
Wire the three computation layers so FLAT positions flow through the full pipeline: resolved by exit price, included in scoring extraction, and scored with correct CLV.

Purpose: A FLAT trader who bought YES at 0.40 and sold at 0.70 currently gets pnl=0 and is filtered from scoring. After this plan they get pnl=size*(0.70-0.40), are extracted, and get CLV=(0.70-0.40)/0.40=0.75.

Output: resolution.py resolves FLAT positions via exit price; extraction.py returns avg_exit_price+direction; metrics.py calculate_clv uses avg_exit_price for FLAT.
</objective>
